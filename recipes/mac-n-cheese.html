Homemade Mac And Cheese

Description

This mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with a roux and adding milk, Cheddar, and Parmesan, resulting in a rich, decadent sauce that coats every nook and cranny of the noodles. Serve this comfort-food macaroni and cheese casserole with grilled meats, sloppy Joes, fried chicken or alongside a salad for a meatless dinner.

Ingredients

  • A box of uncooked macaroni noodles
  • Butter and flour + two tablespoons of butter for the topping
  • Whole milk
  • Cheddar and Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper for sauce, paprika for topping
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
